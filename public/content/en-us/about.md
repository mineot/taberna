# About Taberna

## A place that welcomes ideas

A tavern is a meeting place: everyone arrives with a story, takes a seat, and adds something to the room. The project carries that name because it follows the same idea. It provides a shared structure, then comes to life through the content and identity of the person using it.

Taberna is a customizable foundation for personal websites, portfolios, landing pages, and small institutional sites. Rather than prescribing a single kind of page, it combines flexible sections that can introduce a person, a body of work, an initiative, or a business.

## Simple where it matters

Content is kept separate from the interface. Long-form text is written in Markdown, while menus, sections, images, and language settings are defined in JSON files. This keeps the structure organized and allows the content to evolve without getting lost inside application components.

That simplicity does not mean giving up thoughtful details. The project includes responsive navigation, multiple languages, safe Markdown rendering, accessible components, and a theme built around semantic colors.

## For different stories

Taberna can serve as a starting point for:

- sharing a personal or professional journey;
- gathering projects into a portfolio;
- explaining what a small business has to offer;
- publishing a creative or community initiative;
- building a direct and welcoming landing page.

## A foundation, not a finished identity

The project is not meant to decide what you should say. It organizes the space so your own message can stand out. Titles, copy, images, colors, and links are only examples until you turn them into something of your own.
